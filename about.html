About Us

iChildBloom is Pakistan’s first legally registered autism and disability support software, operating in full compliance with national regulations. As autism prevalence grows, we empower families and professionals with innovative tools to monitor developmental progress, deliver structured guidance, and maintain engagement throughout the intervention journey. Our platform equips parents and tutors with evidence-based assessments, progress-tracking features, and motivational resources—all designed to support consistent, data-driven care. For organizations, we offer scalable solutions to streamline autism screening, staff management, and outcome measurement. Built by experts and rooted in local needs, iChildBloom is pioneering inclusive support systems to help Pakistan’s neurodiverse community thrive.
